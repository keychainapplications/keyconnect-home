KeyConnect+ is a sports, events, and social networking app developed and operated by an individual developer. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, shared, and protected through our website (www.keychainapps.net), mobile applications, and related services (collectively, the "Services"). By using the Services, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1 About KeyConnect+ and Our Users

KeyConnect+ provides a digital platform to manage and participate in sports teams, events, and social networking. Our Services are organized around the following user types:

  • Registered User — a user who creates a KeyConnect+ account with a name, email address, and password to access password-protected features
  • Team Admin / Organization — a Registered User with administrator access to manage one or more teams, events, or organizations
  • Player — a sports participant over the age of 13, or the parent/guardian of a youth participant under 13
  • Household Member — another member of a Player's household who uses the Services
  • Fan — a user who accesses only public portions of the Services that do not require an account

Users can post, publish, store, display, stream, and share information about Players, Teams, Events, and other content ("User Content"), including Player data, photos, audio, and video ("Media Content").

2 How We Collect Information

We may collect information about you by various means, including:

  • Directly from you, both online (website, App) and offline
  • Through your use of our Services
  • From Team Admins, Organizations, and other Users who provide information about you
  • From third-party sources and social media platforms you use to engage with us

3 Categories of Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide

  • Registered User information such as name, email address, password, mailing address, phone number, and subscription preferences
  • Player information such as name, birthday, gender, email address, mailing address, and phone number
  • Team and event information including team name, location, time zone, sport type, and event details
  • Contact list information (with your prior permission) to invite friends to the App
  • Profile information including photos, bio, sport preferences, and event interests
  • Survey or feedback responses
  • User Content posted to the Services by you or other Users
  • Communications with us via email, phone, in-app messaging, or other channels
  • Chat information when you use team chat and direct messaging features (conversations are recorded and may be shared with our chat service provider)

3.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage details including date, time, length of visits, pages or content accessed, search terms, and frequency of visits
  • Device information including IP address, device type, unique device identifier, operating system, browser type, and mobile network information
  • Location information including general location via IP address and, where you grant permission, precise geolocation from your device

3.3 Information From Team Admins, Organizations, and Other Users

Team Admins and Organizations may provide us with User Content and personal information about you, including eligibility, roster details, scheduling, and performance information. We do not generally control how Team Admins and Organizations input, use, or share this content.

3.4 Anonymized Information

We may collect or convert information into anonymized or aggregate format that does not identify you. Such information may be used for analytics, marketing, and service improvement.

4 Online User Activity & Tracking Technologies

We and our vendors use cookies, pixels, SDKs, and similar technologies ("Activity Tools") to collect Online User Activity when you use our Services. Activity Tools help us identify returning users, analyze traffic patterns, improve the Services, and deliver customized content and advertising.

Most browsers allow you to reject cookies. If you disable cookies, some features of the Services may not function correctly. We endeavor to honor Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals from supported browsers.

5 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide our Services to you, including maintaining your account and facilitating engagement with Teams and other Users
  • Enable team and organization management, event organization, roster management, and scheduling
  • Allow you to post, share, and interact with content and other users
  • Send transactional communications such as account alerts, event notifications, and service updates
  • Send promotional communications and newsletters (where you have opted in)
  • Respond to your requests, questions, and comments and provide customer support
  • Monitor performance metrics and analyze and improve the Services
  • Personalize your experience and deliver relevant content and advertising
  • Protect the security and integrity of our Services and prevent fraud
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

6.3 Legal, Security, and Safety

We may disclose your information if required by law, to prevent harm or financial loss, in connection with suspected fraudulent or illegal activity, or to protect the safety of our users or the public.

6.4 Business Transfers

6.5 Advertising Partners

We may share aggregated and/or anonymized personal information with advertising partners. You can opt out via the cookie banner on our website. Some advertisers may be members of the NAI or DAA.

6.6 With Your Consent

We may share your information for other purposes with your explicit consent.

7 Your Privacy Choices

7.1 Marketing Emails

You may opt out of marketing emails at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any such email. We will continue sending non-marketing communications such as account alerts.

7.2 Text Messages

If you have opted in to receive text messages, you may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for assistance. Message and data rates may apply. Opting out by other means may result in delays.

7.3 Account Information

You may update your account information through the App settings or request deletion by contacting us at privacy@keychainapps.net.

7.4 Location Data

You may disable precise location access through your device settings at any time. We may still collect general location from your IP address.

7.5 Cookies

You may manage cookie preferences through your browser settings or our in-app cookie controls.

7.6 App Notifications

You may opt out of push notifications by adjusting permissions in your mobile device settings or by uninstalling the App.

8 How to Update Your Information

  • Log in to your account and update your profile and communication preferences directly
  • Contact us at privacy@keychainapps.net to request updates to your account information
  • Contact the relevant Team Admin or Organization to request changes to User Content or information associated with a team or player

We may refuse requests that are unreasonably repetitive, require disproportionate technical efforts, risk the privacy of others, or are impractical.

9 Children's Privacy

KeyConnect+ is intended for users 13 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without express parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have collected unauthorized information about your child under 13, please contact us at privacy@keychainapps.net and we will promptly delete it.

If you are a California resident under the age of 18, please do not share personal information on any public content channel we offer. If you do post such information, you may request removal by contacting us.

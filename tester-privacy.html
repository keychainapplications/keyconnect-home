1 Who Is a Tester?

A "Tester" is any individual who has been invited to access a pre-release or beta version of KeyConnect+ through:

  • Google Play Internal Testing track
  • Google Play Closed Testing (Alpha) track
  • Google Play Open Testing (Beta) track
  • Direct APK distribution by the developer

By accepting an invitation and installing a test build of KeyConnect+, you agree to the terms of this Tester Privacy Policy in addition to the main Privacy Policy.

You must be 13 years of age or older to participate in the KeyConnect+ testing program. By joining, you confirm you meet this requirement.

In addition to data collected from regular users, we collect the following from testers:

3 How We Use Tester Data

Data collected from testers is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Bug identification — finding and fixing crashes, errors, and unexpected behavior
  • Performance improvement — optimizing app speed, memory usage, and battery consumption
  • Feature validation — understanding how testers interact with new features
  • UX improvement — identifying confusing flows or friction points in the user experience
  • Compatibility testing — ensuring the app works correctly across different Android devices and versions
✅ Tester data is never used for advertising, marketing, or sold to third parties.

4 Crash Reports & Diagnostics

KeyConnect+ uses Firebase Crashlytics to automatically collect crash reports when the app encounters an unexpected error. This is enabled by default for test builds.

What a crash report contains:

  • The line of code that caused the crash (stack trace)
  • App version and build number
  • Android OS version and device model
  • Time of crash
  • Whether the app was in the foreground or background

What a crash report does NOT contain:

  • Your name or email address
  • Your precise location
  • Any message content or personal data you entered
  • Photos or files from your device
Firebase Crashlytics is operated by Google. For more information on how Google handles this data, see the Firebase Privacy Policy.

5 Feedback & Bug Reports

As a tester, you may be asked to submit feedback through:

  • Google Play's in-app feedback form
  • Email to keychainapps@gmail.com
  • Any other feedback channel provided by the developer

When you submit feedback:

  • Your email address is collected so we can follow up
  • Any screenshots or recordings you attach are stored securely
  • Feedback content is only read by the developer and not shared publicly
  • Your feedback may be used to improve the app but will never be attributed to you publicly
⚠ Please do not include sensitive personal information (passwords, financial data, government IDs) in feedback submissions or bug reports.

6 Data Sharing

Tester data may be shared only with the following trusted service providers, strictly for testing purposes:

Service Purpose Data Shared
Firebase Crashlytics (Google) Crash reporting Device info, crash logs
Firebase Analytics (Google) Feature usage analytics Anonymized usage events
Google Play Console Test distribution & feedback Email (for testers only)

We do not share tester data with advertisers, data brokers, or any other third parties.

7 Data Retention

Tester data is retained as follows:

  • Crash logs — retained for 90 days after the test build expires
  • Performance metrics — retained for 90 days
  • Feedback submissions — retained until the issue is resolved or for 1 year, whichever comes first
  • Account data — retained for 30 days after you leave the testing program, then permanently deleted
When KeyConnect+ launches publicly, test accounts will not be automatically migrated. You will need to create a new account in the production app.

8 Confidentiality

As a tester, you may have access to unreleased features, designs, and functionality that are not yet available to the public. By participating in the testing program, you agree to:

  • Not share screenshots, recordings, or descriptions of unreleased features publicly
  • Not post about the test build on social media without prior written permission
  • Not share your test invitation link with others who were not invited
  • Keep any pre-release information confidential until the feature is publicly launched
⚠ Violation of confidentiality may result in removal from the testing program and revocation of your test access.

9 Your Rights as a Tester

You have the following rights regarding your data as a tester:

Access

You can request a copy of the data we hold about you by emailing keychainapps@gmail.com.

Deletion

You can request deletion of all your tester data at any time. We will process your request within 30 days. Use our account deletion page or email us directly.

Opt out of analytics

You can opt out of analytics collection within the app under Settings → Privacy → Analytics. Note that crash reporting cannot be disabled in test builds as it is essential for identifying issues.

Correction

If any data we hold about you is incorrect, contact us and we will correct it promptly.

10 Leaving the Program

You can leave the KeyConnect+ testing program at any time:

Via Google Play:

  1. Open Google Play Store on your Android device
  2. Search for KeyConnect+
  3. Scroll down to the "Join the beta" section
  4. Tap Leave the programme
  5. Uninstall the test build

Via email:

Email keychainapps@gmail.com with subject line "Remove me from KeyConnect+ testing" and we will remove you within 24 hours.

✅ Upon leaving, your tester account data will be deleted within 30 days. Anonymized crash logs may be retained for up to 90 days as described in Section 7.
